Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum number of guests for catering? +
The minimum is 20 guests for our standard packages. For custom events with fewer guests, please contact us directly for a special quote.
Do you offer dietary accommodations? +
Absolutely! We can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other dietary requirements. Please mention this in your request form.
Can we customize the menu? +
Yes! We offer fully customizable menus. Our chef will work with you to create the perfect menu for your event.
What is the advance notice required? +
We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance. For larger events or custom requests, please allow 4 weeks if possible.
